About Our Firm

Clear River Partners is a specialized investment firm dedicated to identifying undervalued companies in the mid-market sector. Our approach combines rigorous financial analysis with deep industry expertise to uncover opportunities that others may overlook.

We focus on acquiring well-positioned businesses with strong fundamentals that are trading below their intrinsic value. Through strategic improvements and operational optimization, we enhance these assets before presenting them to qualified buyers.

Our team brings decades of combined experience in investment banking, private equity, and corporate development, providing the expertise necessary to execute complex transactions and create substantial value for all stakeholders.
